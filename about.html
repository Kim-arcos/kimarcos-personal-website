These are some of my favorite pasttimes When I'm not writing and editing code.

Cooking

I've always been co-chef to my mom growing up. I started cooking on my own since I was 13 years old and my skills and recipes have really improved and broadened. I like to take inspirtation from the internet and follow other peoples recipes. Changing them a bit to add my own spice.

I perfer to make food at home, because when it comes down to it, it is a lot healthier and cheaper. Even though most of my recipes are mexican styled, I like to make pasta with homemade sauce. The only thing I think that I can't make would be my mother's salsa. Her's is so good and always has the perfect amount of heat.

Playing Music

I've been in each of my schools bands since I was in third grade. I learned to play violin, saxophone, clarinet, guitar, and the piano. I like to listen to classical pieces by Vivaldi and Beethoven when I'm cleaning or relaxing.

When I was in school I took advantage of the free lessons and practice time after school. I learned a lot and was able to improve my music skills drastically. Even though I don't play as much as I use to, I still like to pick up my guitar and learn some new songs. I even started to teach my siblings how to play the piano.

Going to the Gym

I like to work out on my non-work days. I try to aim to going to the gym three times a week. On the days that I can't go, I still squeeze a workout in at home. Over the years I've bought some dumbbells and other gym equipment for that purpose.

Going to the gym and staying consistent has really improved my mental and physical health. I feel great and have energy throughout my entire day. It also improves my eating habits.
